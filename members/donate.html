Support the April Society

We appreciate your interest in contributing to the work of the April Society. At this time, however, we are unable to accept formal donations on behalf of the organization.

The Society is currently operating as an unincorporated association. We intend to register soon as an Oklahoma nonprofit and pursue 501(c)(7) status to allow for responsible fundraising in compliance with state and federal law.

If you are interested in supporting the Society financially, please speak directly with a current member or director who may provide further guidance.
